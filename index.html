The Moshaz Story

Two visionaries, one house of beauty.

Founded in 2004 by Shazia & Mona — internationally trained beauty specialists who refined their craft across France, Singapore and Dubai before bringing world-class glamour home to Pakistan.

For more than two decades, Moshaz has been the name brides, professionals and women across Rawalpindi and Islamabad trust for flawless, personalised results. Our artists work as one team — striving to make every visit feel effortless, every result feel like you, perfected.
